About Scarfia

Our Scarfia founders set out to create the accessory their outfits so desperately needed, and the signature scarfia duo scarf was born. Within 1 year, the scarfia brand grew to include more than 20 designs in over 15 collections – all with one thing in common: each was designed from scratch and handcrafted specifically for Scarfia. With every collection launched, our founders still continue to try on the scarves themselves, to ensure that our customers receive the premium quality we know they deserve. So all we’re really trying to say here is… Scarfia was created out of a desire to revive modesty, and continues to exist in all the ways that it does because of you, our beloved lady in Scarfia, and we couldn’t be more grateful. After all, Scarfia is more than just an online brand. We are a community.
